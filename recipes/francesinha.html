Francesinha Recipe

This is a recipe for the legendary sandwich of Porto.

Ingredients needed:

  • bread
    • cheese
  • ham
  • beers
  • steak
  • ketchup

Steps:

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Grill the beef
  3. Fry the cips
  4. Bake the bread
  5. Assemble the sammich
  6. Cover with slices of cheese
  7. Add the boiling sauce
