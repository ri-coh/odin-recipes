Simple Roastbeef Recipe!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Whatever you want to add
  1. Smash a few garlic cloves
  2. Add them to a frying pan with the hot oil
  3. Add the rosemary beads
  4. Season the Beef generously with pepper and salt
  5. Add the Beef once it starts to smell like garlic
  6. Fry each side quickly for a min or two
  7. Bake in the oven for 5-8mins
